TARGET-AUDIENCE

The Bogota Chamber of Commerce caters to individuals and organizations looking to establish or expand their business operations in Bogota. Our target audience includes entrepreneurs, small, medium, and large business owners, investors, and other stakeholders interested in the economic development of Bogota.

Two personas

  1. A successful business owner in her 40s, with experience in the healthcare industry, is looking to expand her business to Bogota. She is interested in learning about the local market, the regulatory environment, and the support available to businesses in the area.
  2. A recent college graduate with a degree in marketing, looking to start a digital marketing agency in Bogota. She is keen to understand the local business landscape, identify potential clients, and network with other entrepreneurs and business owners in the area.

Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1: Persona #1 wants to know about the upcoming business events in Bogota, particularly ribbon-cutting events. She is interested in leveraging such events to market her business. She reaches out to the Chamber of Commerce and learns about the various networking events and opportunities available for businesses to promote their brand.
  2. Scenario 2: Persona #1 wants to know which companies are part of the Chamber of Commerce in Bogota. She is interested in networking and partnering with other businesses in the area. She visits the Chamber's website and browses the member directory to identify potential collaborators.
  3. Scenario 3: Persona #2 wants to understand the membership tiers available at the Chamber of Commerce in Bogota. She is interested in the marketing benefits offered by each tier and whether all community events are open for Chamber members to promote their brand. She reaches out to the Chamber of Commerce and learns about the various membership tiers, the benefits offered, and how to join.
  4. Scenario 4: Persona #2 wants to network with other business owners in Bogota. She is interested in collaborating with other entrepreneurs to expand her client base. She visits the Chamber's website and discovers various networking events, such as business luncheons, seminars, and workshops. She attends these events and connects with like-minded business owners.
  5. Scenario 5: Persona #2 wants to identify community events where she could promote her business. She is interested in leveraging local events to expand her brand's reach. She visits the Chamber's website and discovers a calendar of events that lists community events, festivals, and fairs happening in Bogota. She identifies events relevant to her business and starts planning her marketing strategy.
